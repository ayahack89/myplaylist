Vibelist

Vibelist is a fun, frontend-only music experience I built for my own playlist collection. It's a simple way to browse, explore, and enjoy tracks I love, without the hassle of digging through endless lists. You'll find a mix of chill vibes, groovy tunes, and high-energy hits, all in one place.

It's not connected to any streaming service, just a personal space to share the music I enjoy. Feel like exploring? Click Explore and start the vibe.
