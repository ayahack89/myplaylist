About Me

Introduction

Hello — I am Ayanabha Chatterjee, a 19-year-old developer and the creator of this project. I built this site to collect and organise my favourite tracks and short previews in a simple, frontend-first application.

Website Overview

Vibelist is a personal music playground for browsing curated playlists and short preview clips. The site is intended as a lightweight, educational/demo project and is inspired by modern streaming interfaces. It is not affiliated with the commercial streaming services.

Features

You can browse tracks, save songs to your personal playlist, and play short preview clips. Some tracks may offer preview streams; for full-length playback, please use licensed streaming providers.

Known Issues

When playing multiple tracks in different areas of the app, audio can overlap if an existing preview is not paused before starting another. Please pause the currently playing preview before starting a new one. This is a UI/UX limitation, note for now and may be improved in later updates.
